Videography is the art and process of capturing moving images or video footage using a video camera or other recording devices. Videographers often focus on creating dynamic visual narratives, documentaries, films, or promotional videos. It involves the use of techniques such as framing, composition, lighting, and editing to convey stories and messages effectively through motion and sound.

Photography is the art of capturing still images or photographs using a camera. Photographers use their creative visionand knowledge of light and composition to freeze moments in time. Photography encompasses a wide range of genres, including portrait, landscape, product, fashion, and more. Photographers aim to evoke emotions or simply capture the beauty of a scene through a single frame.
